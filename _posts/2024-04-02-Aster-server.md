This instruction will show how to connect Aster server to Visual Studio Code.
<!--more-->

__NOTE__: Please don’t forget to connect through VPN, if you need it.
**Step 1: Login to server through ssh in terminal.** 

<img width="748" alt="image" src="https://github.com/xin-wang-kr/xin-wang-kr.github.io/assets/28020765/2ed6eef6-b45c-4dc5-82b9-0ef1efc6be02">


**Step 2: Open Visual Studio Code and click “Connect to…”.**

<img width="750" alt="image" src="https://github.com/xin-wang-kr/xin-wang-kr.github.io/assets/28020765/f60681b0-6628-443e-85b9-dda415bca15f">


**Step 3: Select “Connect to Host…”.**

<img width="749" alt="image" src="https://github.com/xin-wang-kr/xin-wang-kr.github.io/assets/28020765/bb4ca2e3-1239-4ab3-bcdc-30f9d18c1ddd">


**Step 4: Enter user@host and then press "Enter".**

<img width="750" alt="image" src="https://github.com/xin-wang-kr/xin-wang-kr.github.io/assets/28020765/92e66cf6-b6be-43aa-b3bb-eb0c24253a97">


**Step 5: Enter your password for your account.**

<img width="748" alt="image" src="https://github.com/xin-wang-kr/xin-wang-kr.github.io/assets/28020765/c6f39b5f-b6cd-42ef-a404-5334473464bc">

**Step 6: After successfully connect to server, click “Explorer” at left top conner to connect your folder from server (or clone repository, it depends on your need.).**

<img width="749" alt="image" src="https://github.com/xin-wang-kr/xin-wang-kr.github.io/assets/28020765/ef67957e-eb61-4c8f-8826-c6f6f55cf629">

You will be asked to type your password. After that, you are __DONE__!
